Reason 2 of 4

Security.

iOS removes the failure modes AI is most likely to make.
Migrapulse runs on device. No server. No breach surface.
Database open to the internet.
Default-public schemas. Missing row-level rules. AI ships it; an SQL string finds it.
Auth bypassed in a weekend.
A teenager finds a missing check. The forgot-password flow is the front door.
One bad migration — data gone.
Vibe a schema change. Ship it. Wake up to wiped histories you can't restore.
